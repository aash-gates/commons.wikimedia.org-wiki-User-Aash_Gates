ವಿಕಿಮೀಡಿಯ ಕಾಮನ್ಸ್ ಪಂಡ ದಾದ ?

ವಿಕಿಮೀಡಿಯ ಕಾಮನ್ಸ್ ಪಂಡ media file repository making available public domain and freely-licensed educational media content (images, sound and video clips) to everyone, ಎಕ್ಲ ಭಾಸೆಡ್ ನಿಯಮೊಲ್. It acts as a common repository for the various projects of the ವಿಕಿಮೀಡಿಯ ಪೌಂಡೇಶನ್, but you do not need to belong to one of those projects to use media hosted here. The repository is created and maintained not by paid archivists, but by volunteers. The scope of Commons is set out on the ಯೋಜನೆತ ಸ್ವರೂಪೊ pages.

Wikimedia Commons uses the same wiki-technology as ವಿಕಿಪೀಡಿಯ and everyone can edit it. Unlike media files uploaded to other projects, files uploaded to Wikimedia Commons can be embedded on pages of all Wikimedia projects without the need to separately upload them there.

7 ಸಪ್ಟಂಬರ್ 2004 ಸುರು ಅಯಿನ, ವಿಕಿಮೀಡಿಯ ಕಾಮನ್ಸ್ hit the 1,000,000 uploaded media file milestone on 30 November 2006 and currently contains 87,583,921 files and 85,605,868 media collections. More background information about the Wikimedia Commons project itself can be found in the General disclaimer, at the ವಿಕಿಪೀಡಿಯ ಪುಟೊಡ್ ವಿಕಿಮೀಡಿಯ ಕಾಮನ್ಸ್ and ಈ ಪುಟೊ ಮೇಟೊಡುಲಾ ಉಂಡು.

Unlike traditional media repositories, Wikimedia Commons is free. Everyone is allowed to copy, use and modify any files here freely as long as they follow the terms specified by the author; this often means crediting the source and author(s) appropriately and releasing copies/improvements under the same freedom to others. The license conditions of each individual media file can be found on their description page. The Wikimedia Commons database itself and the texts in it are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution/Share-Alike License. More information on re-use can be found at Commons:Reusing content outside Wikimedia and Commons:First steps/Reuse.

ಪಾಲ್ ದೆತೊನಕ್ಲು

You can improve Wikimedia Commons most if you contribute what you can do best:

ಇರ್ಲಾ ಸೇರೊಲಿ

If you're a good photographer, don't hesitate to contribute your valuable images. If you're a good designer, look which diagrams and animations are badly needed.

ಇರೆನ ಕೌಶಲ್ಯೊ

However you do not need to upload your own files. There is plenty of other very important work to do:

  • Translate pages ಬೇತೆ ಭಾಸೆಡ್ ಇಂದು ಉಂಡು
  • ಪಟೊ ಸೋಕ್ ಇಪ್ಪೊಡು
  • Identify unknown objects
  • Contribute your legal knowledge on ಕಾಪಿರೈಟ್ ಪ್ರಶ್ನೆಲು and ಮಾಜಾವುನೆ

ಇಂದೆಕ್ ಇರೆನ ಸಮಯ ಕೊರ್ಲೆ

You even do not need to be an artist or good at writing help texts. If you feel at home with creating order from chaos, then we could use your helping hand!

  • Anytime you see a file with incomplete licensing or source information, tag it with {{subst:nsd}}.
  • Give Media needing categories a category.
  • Move files into relevant subcategories
  • Free media resources are waiting for an inclusion into this project.
  • Nominate or vote for Featured Pictures (registration required).
  • Find and revert vandalism

ಪ್ರವಾಸೊ

  • First steps
  • What can I do for Wikimedia Commons?
ನೊಂದಣಿ

In order to be able to upload files on Wikimedia Commons, you need to be logged in. You can register at the "ಲಾಗಿನ್ /ಖಾತೆ ಸುರು ಮಾಲ್ಪುಲೆ" link in the upper right corner and enter a user name that will be used at all of your uploads/edits on images and texts. However if you just want to edit pages you don't need to be logged in (although it is encouraged). If you have taken advantage of unified login, then you are already signed up at Commons.

ಸುರು ಹಂತ ಟುಟೋರಿಯಲ್

Our ಸುರುಗು ಸಹಾಯೊ ಪುಟೊ and our FAQ will help you a lot after registration. They explain how to customize the interface (for example the language), how to ಪಟೊ Upload and our basic ಲೈಸನ್ಸಿಂಗ್ ನಿಯಮ. You don't need technical skills in order to contribute here. Be bold contributing here and assume good faith for the intentions of others. This is a wiki - it is really easy.

More information is available at the ಸಮುದಾಯ ಪುಟು. You may ask questions at the ಚಾವಡಿ or on IRC channel #wikimedia-commons webchat.

Files on Wikimedia Commons are organized in categories and galleries. An overview of the categories we use is available on the Main Page.

Put Babel boxes on your user page so others know what languages you can speak and indicate your graphic abilities. All your uploads are stored in your personal gallery. Please sign your name on Talk pages by typing ~~~~. If you're copying files from another project, be sure to use the CommonsHelper.

ಬೆತೆ ಸೇವೆಲು ಬುಕ್ಕೊ ತಂತ್ರಾಂಶ

If you want to upload large numbers of images, the program VicuñaUploader or Commonist will be helpful. If you need specific information on tools for viewing or editing our content please see the software page and tools page.

We hope that you will enjoy it here and that you will have fun.
