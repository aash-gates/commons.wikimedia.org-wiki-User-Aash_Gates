Design, purpose[edit]

Started at Commons_talk:Requests_for_comment/galleries#Confused

"discussions on policies, guidelines or other matters that have a wide impact and on which a broad consensus is needed" is stated on Commons:Centralized discussion.

Maybe the design of {{Cent}} is not as good(?) as de:Vorlage:Beteiligen? Only the first line (Meinungsbilder) and in the last (Stewardwahlen) is important - the remaining is not really. One may participate but would not miss to "cast his vote" (kind of) if he does not. Once the RFCs were translated with "Meinungsbilder" (in cent) - but apparently they are more like de:WP:PRD (long term discussions).

So I feel that cent does not really fit it's purpose if such unfinished discussions like Commons:Requests_for_comment/galleries are advertised. There is no proposal, no vote, just gathering opinions of the people who participate and nothing will be decided soon. Due to the way the cent template is designed (lightwight, small) there is not much room for some kind of classification. Just maybe some kind of icon could be used. just after the "*". Cheers --Saibo (Δ) 03:09, 20 February 2012 (UTC)Reply[reply]

Image size ratio[edit]

I'd like that every page for an image list the size ratio of the image along with its actual resolution. E.g. if an image is 800x600 pixels, the description will list a ratio of 1.33:1. 

(800 × 600 pixels (1.33:1), file size: 8.85 MB, MIME type: image/jpeg)

This could be useful for editors using multiple image templates and such. Kurzon (talk) 11:13, 24 August 2019 (UTC)Reply[reply]
