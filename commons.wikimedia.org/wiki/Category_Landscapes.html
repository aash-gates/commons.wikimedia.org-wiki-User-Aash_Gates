This is a main category requiring frequent diffusion and maybe maintenance. As many pictures and media files as possible should be moved into appropriate subcategories.
In a non-practical sense almost anything outdoors can be called a landscape. A landscape therefore in this category is a combination of different landforms forming a new picture which cannot be categorized as one of the subcategories of Category:Landforms. The picture should cover at least a few square kilometers to be called a landscape.
  • The term Landscape refers to the type of image, geographical entities refer to Category:Regions. For landscape as a theme, see Category:Landscape (environment).
  • Especially buildings, mountains and forests do NOT belong into this category. Refer to Category:Buildings, Category:Mountains and Category:Forests in this case.
  • More landscape photo on OpenPhoto. Warning: look the license of each image if you want to upload it in Wikimedia.
