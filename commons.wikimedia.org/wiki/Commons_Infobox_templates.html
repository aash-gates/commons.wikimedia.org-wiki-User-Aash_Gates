Commons:Infobox templates

Shortcut: COM:IT

Files on Commons use a variety of infoboxes to display file metadata, with different template customized for different types of files. Infoboxes serve several purposes, like organizing the data and displaying it using unified look, adding machine-readable metadata, and localizing (translating) the field names to the language of the viewer. The main infoboxes which are meant to be used primarily in file namespace are:

  • {{Information}} – default template meant especially for photographs created by users.
  • {{Artwork}} – for paintings, artworks and artifacts held by museums and other GLAM institutions
  • {{Photograph}} – for photographs held by museums and other GLAM institutions
  • {{Art photo}} – for photographs of artworks with more fields for photographer metadata
  • {{Book}} – for books
  • {{Map}} – for maps
  • {{Musical work}} – for music files

There are also many less frequently used templates which were written based on one of the main infobox templates:

  • {{Information2}} – {{Information}} with extra "location" field
  • {{COAInformation}} – for coat of arms files
  • {{Bus-Information}} – for information about buses
  • {{Infobox aircraft image}} – for information about aircraft
  • {{Spoken article}} – for audio files of people reading wikipedia articles
  • {{Specimen}} – for images of biological specimens with known scientific names
