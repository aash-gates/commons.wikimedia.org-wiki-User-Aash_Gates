This category collects subcategories for different languages that our project-related pages (as opposed to content-related pages, such as image galleries) have been translated into. Category:Commons-en can be used as an example category hierarchy for other languages to follow.

Pages should be placed into appropriate subcategories by using the wiki syntax [[Category:Commons-ISO-LANGUAGE-CODE]].
