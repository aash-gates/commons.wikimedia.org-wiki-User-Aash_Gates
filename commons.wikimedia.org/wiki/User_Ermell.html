Hello,

my name is Reinhold Möller (Moeller). Until my retirement in 2016, I was working with a touring symphony orchestra as a musician. Ever since I was a child, I have been interested in photography.

All of my pictures are licensed under a Creative Commons license, so anybody can use them. I believe the majority of my photos deserves to be shared rather than left in storage on a hard drive.
