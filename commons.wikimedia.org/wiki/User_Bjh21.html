My canonical home is elsewhere.

On Commons, I mostly curate images copied here from Geograph Britain and Ireland. To that end, I operate the Geograph Update Bot (contribs). There are some of my photos here, mostly from Geograph but some uploaded directly.

I also operate the Usage Bot (contribs), which has nothing to do with Geograph but instead tracks external file usage.

Here is a gallery of Geograph and Photograph of the Year.

I have a collection of interesting Geograph DRs.
