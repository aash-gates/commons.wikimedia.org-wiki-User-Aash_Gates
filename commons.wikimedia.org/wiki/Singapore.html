Singapore is a city state at the southern tip of peninsular Malaysia. It is an island approximately 40 by 20 km in size inhabited by more than five million people. Residents are mainly Chinese, Malay, Indian and Eurasian. Famous visitor attractions in Singapore include the Merlion statue, the Night Safari, Singapore Zoo, Sentosa Island, Pulau Ubin, Jurong Bird Park, and the Singapore Botanic Gardens.
