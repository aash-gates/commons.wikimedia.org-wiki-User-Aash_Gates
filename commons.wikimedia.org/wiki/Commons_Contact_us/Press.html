Press
If you're a member of the press looking to contact Wikimedia Commons, or have a business proposal for us


You've got mics, we've got voices

If you are a member of the press looking to contact the Wikimedia Foundation, their press department can be contacted at presswikimedia.org , or by phoning +1 415-839-6885. We also have international contacts, some of whom work for various affiliated organisations and some of whom are editors. Our press room contains more information, along with materials about Wikimedia Commons, Wikipedia, the Wikimedia Foundation, and other projects it hosts.

If you work for an organisation that is interested in a cultural partnership with Wikimedia Commons, Wikipedia, the Wikimedia Foundation, or one of its affiliated bodies, there is an outreach page on cultural partnerships where you can propose new ones or get in touch with experienced outreach volunteers. For general questions about the Wikimedia Foundation or its work, please email answerswikimedia.org .
