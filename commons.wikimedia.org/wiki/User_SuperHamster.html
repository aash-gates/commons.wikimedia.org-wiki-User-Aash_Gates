About Me

Hey there — I'm Kevin, a 26-year-old Wikimedian currently living in Dallas, Texas, United States, where I work as a software engineer. I'm originally from Ohio, and am an active member of the Ohio Wikimedians User Group. I am also on the board of Wikimedia DC.

I mainly contribute to Wikipedia under the name "SuperHamster" (see my userpage here). Here on the Commons, I upload my own photography and also do some light behind-the-scenes work. I am also one of the organizers of Wiki Loves Monuments in the United States since 2016.

Feel free to contact me on my talk page. You may also email me for more private matters. Thank you!
