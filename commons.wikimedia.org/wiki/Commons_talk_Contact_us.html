Suggested change[edit]

Currently, the page reads:

== Report abuse ==
=== Copyright violations ===

  • In order to get quick help with copyright violations or similar issues, you can send an email to a team of volunteer helpers of the project: info-en-c@wikimedia.org. Please state:
    • The precise reason of your inquiry.
    • The page(s) or image(s) of concern.
    • Please note that this is an informal but direct and discreet contact with respect to legal matters provided by volunteers, for official contact see Commons:General disclaimer.
  • If you prefer to use the process of a formal DMCA/OCILLA request, you can send it to the Wikimedia Foundation's designated agent.


===Inappropriate images of children===

  • To report inappropriate images of children, email the Wikimedia Foundation at legal-reportswikimedia.org, being sure to include a link to the content. We do not encourage community members to download or archive potentially inappropriate content.
Suggested change

== Report problem images ==
For all problem reports, please include a link to the images (or pages) of concern, and an explanation of the problem.
=== Copyright violations ===

  • For quick help with copyright violations or related issues, you can email the support team at: info-en-c@wikimedia.org
    This is an informal but fast and discreet contact provided by volunteers.
  • To file a formal DMCA/OCILLA request, send it to the Wikimedia Foundation's designated agent.
  • For other official contacts, see Commons:General disclaimer.


===Inappropriate images of children===
To report inappropriate images of children, email the Wikimedia Foundation at legal-reportswikimedia.org.

Be sure to include a link to the content.
We do not encourage community members to download or archive potentially inappropriate content.

=== Images of yourself ===
If you find an image or video of yourself used without your consent, or simply want to request removal of an image of you:

  • For quick help, you can email the support team at: info-commons@wikimedia.org.
    This is an informal but fast and discreet contact provided by volunteers.
  • Removal of media is not guaranteed. Generally media taken in a private place that fail to honor the subject's right of privacy are removed.
    Other requests for removal, such as courtesy deletion, do not yet have a uniform policy and are reviewed case-by-case.
  • For an official contact see Commons:General disclaimer.
Comments welcome. --SJ+ 22:41, 13 December 2013 (UTC)Reply[reply]

{{Editrequest}}

Images of yourself[edit]

If you find an image or video of yourself published here and want it removed you may request it be deleted. It is recommended you read our deletion policy before making a request. In general you may request a deletion if:

  • The media was published without your consent. The policy at COM:IDENT details this issue. If you believe this policy has not been respected you can raise a deletion request.
  • If you would like the media to be removed for other non-policy based reasons it may be removed as a courtesy.
  • Removal of media is not guaranteed. While media taken in a private place that fail to honor the subject's right of privacy are removed, other requests for removal do not yet have a uniform policy and are reviewed case-by-case.
  • For deletions where you require more discretion than would be provided through a normal deletion request, you may contact our trusted volunteer team for help: info-commons@wikimedia.org.
  • For an official contact see Commons:General disclaimer.
