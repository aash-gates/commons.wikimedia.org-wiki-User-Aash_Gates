@Marchjuly: Uploader Lefjag filed {{Db-g7}} timely in this edit 02:53, 19 August 2022 (UTC), just 3d13h41m after uploading 13:12, 15 August 2022‎ (UTC). Of the two, I prefer File:General Yahya Khan in 1966.jpg because it has better white balance and higher resolution, while KhalqiRevolutionary's File:Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan.jpg has no more redeeming qualities, so I tagged File:Agha Muhammad Yahya Khan.jpg as a scaled-down duplicate. No, uploading the second file was not necessary.   — Jeff G. ツ please ping or talk to me 00:01, 10 October 2022 (UTC)Reply[reply]
Thank you for taking a look at these files. What do you think about the Flickr account claiming the file as "All right reserved"? There's very little information provided about the photo beyond what's written in the Flickr description and the Commons file's description. @Marchjuly: It seems from the flickr album about him that Yahya Khan was a General, then became President in 1969, then was deposed and imprisoned in 1971.
