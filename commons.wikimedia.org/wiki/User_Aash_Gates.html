Aash Gates[edit]

Aashik Jaquilin Krishnan (born 28th August 1997), Known as Aash Gates in Social Media, is a Dual Degree Engineer, Geek, Photographer, YouTuber and a Team Lead at Victory Computers, a unit of Victory Organization which was Co-Founded by him at the age of 11.

Education[edit]

Aash Completed his High School Education from Jubilee School, a School Managed by Kerala Samajam Dooravaninagar, He later Joined SJES Polytechnic, a college recognized by Department of Technical Education Karnataka, and Completed his Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering Later he went on to do Bachelors and completed his Engineering in Information Science from East Point College of Engineering and Technology, a College recognized by Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU)

Interests[edit]

Aash is a Geek Since Windows Vista Era, He maintains the reputation of being a Tech Guy among his peers for the help and fix he does with Technical related things, He is Passionate about Cloud, Virtualization, Networking, and the Open Source Community, He has more than 36,000 Code Contribution to his GitHub Account which counts as one of the most contributed Account of GitHub

Aash also published his research paper on building an Affordable Server for Source Code Control using Raspberry Pi, A Project was also created by him to prove his Research. The Project won the Best Project award and was also selected and funded by Karnataka State Council for Science and Technology.

Certifications[edit]

Aash is a Certified VMWare Technical Associate, and He owns 400 plus Course Completion Certificates from Online Education Platforms like Coursera, Udemy etc. Aash also has 200 plus badges in his Credly account, Credly a Pearson company that hosts the largest and most-connected digital credential network.
