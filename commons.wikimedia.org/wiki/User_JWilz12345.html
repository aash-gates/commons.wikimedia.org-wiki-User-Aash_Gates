Freedom of panorama

  • To all Wikimedia Commons users/contributors: please categorize newly-created FOP-related deletion requests to any of the "/pending" subcategories under c:Category:FOP-related deletion requests/pending. Also, please add "noinclude" tags to prevent collateral categorization of daily listings (example: <noinclude>[[Category:Philippine FOP cases/pending]]</noinclude>). Hundreds of "orphaned" (uncategorized) FOP case pages have been found by me, especially those of French FOP cases. Thank you!
