Welcome!

This is where one usually tells people a bit about oneself, right? OK, here you go: I made my first edit on the German language Wikipedia in 2007, but it took me two more years until my first upload at Commons. In 2012 I got my beloved K-5, and that's about the time when I began considering Commons as my favourite Wikimedia project. I've contributed quite a few images as well as some SVG graphs and drawings.

Want to use my pictures?[edit]

  • The photographs I create and upload to Wikimedia Commons are usually available under some kind of Creative Commons license. Somewhere on the page that describes the image should be a grey box that summarizes the terms of that license (example). That box also contains a link to the Creative Commons website, where you'll find the full text of the license. Unless you are already familiar with CC licenses, please make sure to read and understand the full legal code of the license, as it contains some important information that is not covered by the summary.
  • If you have any questions concerning re-using my works outside the Wikimedia projects (e.g. in a book/magazine or on your private website) you are welcome to drop me a line at my talk page (preferred) or write me an e-mail.
  • If you would like to use one my works under different conditions, please contact me and I will see what I can do for you.
