This is a place where users can communicate with administrators, or administrators with one another. You can report vandalism, problematic users, or anything else that needs an administrator's intervention. Do not report child pornography or other potentially illegal content here; e-mail legal-reportswikimedia.org instead. If reporting threatened harm to self or others also email emergencywikimedia.org.

Reports that do not suit the vandalism noticeboard may be reported here. Requests for page protection/unprotection could also be requested here.


Other reports that require administrator assistance which do not fit in any of the previous three noticeboards may be reported here. Requests for history merging or splitting should be filed at COM:HMS.

Confusing General and Subcategories[edit]

There is a general category named Category:Film directors from Iran and it has two subcategories Category:Male film directors from Iran and Category:Female film directors from Iran. I don't know should I transfers photo categories from "Category:Film directors from Iran" to male and female sub categories or vice versa?

More confusing point is that en:Category:Iranian film directors in English Wikipedia doesn't have male and female subcategories. Shkuru Afshar (talk) 08:01, 8 October 2022 (UTC)Reply[reply]

I think the latter is no problem.
For the former, the category descriptions should tell whether all directors should be kept in the main category or everybody moved to the subcategories.
The problem with keeping everybody in the main category is that it is not obvious when some of the directors haven't been added to the relevant gender subcategory, and people searching by gender will think we don't have any media about them.
The problem with moving everybody (except the non-binary?) away is that people searching for media without caring about gender will mostly look in the bigger subcategory, which will effectively hide away the women (I suppose that's the smaller one).
Thus, making the subcats creates a problem whichever we choose, and both problems if the intension isn't documented. Anybody creating per gender categories should thus create or apply a solution, preferably an already existing general one. I haven't heard about any in common use.
–LPfi (talk) 16:15, 10 October 2022 (UTC)Reply[reply]

@AqueWiki: Hi, and welcome. I am sorry to inform you that you have triggered Special:AbuseFilter/153 by trying to cross-wiki upload a smaller (<50,000 bytes or <2,000,000 pixels) jpg photo as a new user. The following applies to each photo. You indicated it's your own work. Usually when someone uploads a smaller jpg photo, it's a copyright violation taken from the web. Please upload the full-size original of it per COM:HR, including any metadata, but if you were not the original photographer, that person may need to license it on their official website or social media or send permission via VRT with a carbon copy to you. If you can't get a compliant license, the photo may still be uploaded to English Wikipedia in compliance with en:WP:F or French Wikipedia in compliance with fr:WP:EDP because we don't allow Fair Use here. If you use our Upload Wizard instead, you should be able to that filter.   — Jeff G. ツ please ping or talk to me 13:55, 14 October 2022 (UTC)Reply[reply]
