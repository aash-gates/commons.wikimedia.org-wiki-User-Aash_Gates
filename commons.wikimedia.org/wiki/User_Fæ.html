Projects

Batch upload projects are mostly detailed at Project list, there are a lot of them

Other projects of interest:

  • IA books used time-boxed threaded uploads to cater for WMF server upload bugs for large PDFs, and used OCR via pytesseract to detect redundant cover pages for automatic removal
  • User:Fæ/Wayback adding Wayback Machine archive links for selected past batch upload projects
  • User:Fæ/upscale increasing the resolution of cropped images, even when rotated or irregular, by reverse engineering the crop using OpenCV
  • User:Fæ/Imagehash the use of standard image hashes in Python to find near identical image matches of different resolutions and with different EXIF data
  • Listeria reports for WMLGBT+ /LGBTQBIOFR/not_male, /LGBTQBIOSK
Faebot maintained reports
  • Userlist, list of active and recently inactive editors with more than 10,000 edits on Commons.
  • BLP overwrites, images used on Wikipedia biographies overwritten by less experienced accounts in the last week (useful as a vandalism prevention tool).
  • SignificantReverts, the most overwritten images on Commons with changes in the last week.
