Hi, I’m Minh Nguyễn (Nguyễn Xuân Minh in Vietnamese), a software developer and Stanford alumnus [1] originally from Loveland, Ohio, United States.

I volunteer as a bureaucrat at the Vietnamese Wikipedia and Vietnamese Wiktionary, and as an administrator at the Vietnamese Wikibooks, Vietnamese Wikisource, Wikimedia Meta-Wiki, and Wikipedia 10. I’m also one of the people who keep the main Wikipedia portal and sister project portals up-to-date. (If you know HTML, you can too!)

Outside of Wikipedia, I volunteer contributions to OpenStreetMap and work for Mapbox.
