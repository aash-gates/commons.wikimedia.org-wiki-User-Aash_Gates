English: Users by location
To help people find users in a certain location, please add yourself to this category by adding [[Category:Users in (*your country*)]] to your user page. Then, if necessary, place the newly created category in the continental ones by adding [[Category:Users by country|*Your country*]] to the page. This category and its subcategories are likely to be developed beyond the country level, e.g. users from a certain state, city etc.
