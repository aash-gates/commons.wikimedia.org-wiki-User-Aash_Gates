Clem has a background in politics, computing and education. He has taught in Schools, FE Colleges and Adult Education mainly in the inner city.

Rule book[edit]

Rules of Category naming, and the rules of Gallery naming:

  • Commons:Categories#Category_name and Commons:Naming categories - Category naming policy (the last one in perpetual "proposed" stage)
  • Commons:Galleries#Naming_conventions
  • Commons:By location category scheme to add one more.

To delete a category

  • {{delete|reason}}
  • {{bad name|Category:correct category name}}

To clean images from a dirty category

  • {{Intersect categories|Images from the Geograph British Isles project|Cleveland, Ohio}} into Category:Cleveland, England where the template is placed.
  • Category:Category maintenance templates useful potential toys

To geotag a file

Object location51° 23′ 45.24″ N, 0° 29′ 28.68″ E View all coordinates using: OpenStreetMap

Find a file nearby- look at its geotag (as above)- click on the numbers and then click on Open streetmap - zoom in and click at the target spot- you can pick up the coordinates from the box top left.

  • commons camera location {{location dec|51.3959|0.4913}}
  • commons object location {{object location dec|51.3959|0.4913}}
  • en wikipedia {{Coord|53.494|-2.451|display=inline,title|format=dms|name="Arrowsmiths Mill"}}
  • de wikipedia
