Wiki Loves Monuments (WLM) is a public photo competition around cultural heritage monuments, organized by Wikimedia chapters, groups and local Wikipedia volunteers. The public takes photos of monuments, upload those to Wikimedia Commons, and then they can be used in Wikipedia and elsewhere. The goal is to make the world's heritage monuments visible for the world wide public.

This Wikimedia Commons portal is dedicated to Wiki Loves Monuments. If you want to contribute photographs, you can have a look at wikilovesmonuments.org or directly to Commons upload page.

Wiki Loves Monuments can refer to:

  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2010, a photo competition in the Netherlands in September 2010
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2011, a European photo competition in September 2011
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2012, a worldwide photo competition in September 2012
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2013, a worldwide photo competition in September 2013
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2014, a worldwide photo competition in September 2014
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2015, a worldwide photo competition in September 2015
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2016, a worldwide photo competition in September 2016
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2017, a worldwide photo competition in September 2017
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2018, a worldwide photo competition in September 2018
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2019, a worldwide photo competition in September 2019
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2020, a worldwide photo competition in September-November 2020
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2021, a worldwide photo competition in September-October 2021
  • Wiki Loves Monuments 2022, a worldwide photo competition in September-October 2022
